Custom Domain Name on Github Pages

I have had the domain cmuthambiri.me for two years now.

Linking it to work perfectly with my GitHub Pages took a little bit of messing around.

Created a CNAME page with my domain name 
$ cmuthambiri.me

I could now see pages being published to cmuthambiri.me when i typed muthambiri.github.io

Pointed the www and * records to muthambiri.github.me for reverse publishing when i type www or just cmuthambiri.me
